A Year of Expanding Worlds

Between the ages of three and four, a child's imagination blossoms. They begin to follow more complex stories, understand humor, and develop a deeper sense of empathy for characters. The books in this collection are chosen to nurture this growth, featuring timeless classics, tales of adventure, and stories that reflect a child's everyday life and feelings.

Use the tabs below to explore the curated book list for each month. Click on any book to learn about its significance, and use the chart to see the balance of themes and genres for each period.
